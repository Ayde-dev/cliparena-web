Privacy Policy — ClipArena

Last updated: 5 June 2026

ClipArena ("we", "us") operates social media channels that curate and publish short-form video clips. This policy explains how we handle data in connection with our use of third-party platform APIs (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram).

1. Information we process

We do not collect personal information from viewers of our content. To publish videos to our own social media accounts, we use authorized access (OAuth) provided by the respective platforms. We store only the access and refresh tokens required to publish content to our own accounts, kept locally and never shared.

2. How we use information

Access tokens are used solely to upload and publish video content to our own connected accounts via the platforms' official APIs. We do not access, collect, or store data belonging to other users.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell or share any data with third parties. Content is published only to the platforms you would expect (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram).

4. Data retention

Authorization tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the service and can be revoked at any time through the respective platform's settings.
